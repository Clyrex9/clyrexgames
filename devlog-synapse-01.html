Synapse Beta #01 Announcement July 9, 2026

Meet Synapse

Something different from us. Synapse is an AI-powered interactive fiction and roleplay app — characters that answer in their own voice, stories that bend to your choices, and a community that shares what it creates. It just entered closed beta on Google Play.

DeckBall taught us something we didn't expect: the features players talked about the most weren't the mechanics — they were the stories. The dating app, the NPCs with opinions, the career arcs. People stayed for the fiction. Synapse takes that lesson and builds a whole app around it: no pitch, no cards, just you, a character, and a story that reacts to every line you write.

What Synapse Is
01

Chat & Live the Story

Core Loop

Dive into romantic tension, drama, mystery, and adventure. Every character answers in their own voice, and as the conversation unfolds, the story is shaped by your choices — not a fixed script. No two playthroughs of the same story read the same way.

  • AI characters with distinct voices and personalities
  • Romance, drama, mystery, and adventure genres
  • Stories that branch on your choices, mid-conversation
02

Create Your Own

Creator Tools

Synapse isn't just a library — it's a workshop. Design your own characters and stories, add a cover image, and publish them for everyone. Every published piece goes through moderation before it reaches the community.

  • Character and story builders
  • Custom cover images
  • Publish to the community — with moderation on every submission
03

Community

Social

Discover what other creators are building. Like, save, and comment on stories, follow the creators you enjoy, and build a profile around what you make and play.

  • Discover feed for stories and characters
  • Like, save, follow, and comment
  • Creator profiles
04

Built Safely

Under the Hood

Synapse is a soft 16+ experience: romantic tension and drama are encouraged, explicit sexual content is banned, and content passes through AI moderation. On the technical side, no API keys ever ship inside the app — every AI request goes through our own secure backend, where credits and limits are enforced server-side.

  • 16+ audience, AI content moderation
  • All AI calls routed through a secure server — no keys in the app
  • Server-side credit and rate limiting
Where It Stands

Synapse is in closed beta on Google Play right now. Google requires new indie developer accounts to run a closed test for at least 14 days with a group of testers before an app can go to production — so that's exactly what we're doing. We're using this window to polish the experience, finish in-app account deletion, and wire up the last pieces before launch.

Want in early? We'd love more testers. Send a quick email to clyrex9@gmail.com with the Google account you use on the Play Store, and we'll add you to the beta list.

What's Next

Between now and the public release: in-app account deletion, credit purchases, push notifications, and another layer of backend hardening. Once the closed test window clears, Synapse goes live on Google Play — and this devlog will be there for every step, same as DeckBall's.
