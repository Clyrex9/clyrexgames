Introduction

Clyrex Games ("we", "us", "our") develops mobile applications including DeckBall: Football Card RPG and Money Clicker Tycoon (collectively, the "Apps"). This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use our Apps.

By using our Apps, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Data We Collect

Depending on which features you use, the following types of data may be collected:

  • Email address & User ID (optional) — Collected only if you choose to sign in with Google. Used for account management and app functionality.
  • Device or other identifiers — Collected automatically for analytics, advertising, and fraud prevention purposes.
  • App interactions — How you interact with the App (screens visited, features used). Used for analytics and improving app functionality.
  • Crash logs & diagnostics — Technical data about app performance and errors. Used to identify and fix bugs.
  • Transaction history — Records of in-app purchases, used for order fulfillment and fraud prevention.

Third-Party Services

Our Apps use the following third-party services that may collect data independently:

  • Google AdMob — Displays advertisements. Collects device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. Google Privacy Policy ↗
  • Firebase Analytics — Provides usage analytics and crash reporting. Collects app interactions, diagnostics, and device identifiers. Firebase Privacy ↗
  • Google Sign-In — Optional authentication method. If used, collects your email address and user ID for account management. Google Privacy Policy ↗
  • Google Play Services — Handles app distribution, updates, and in-app purchases. Google Privacy Policy ↗

Advertising

Our Apps display ads provided by Google AdMob. Google may use device identifiers and usage data to show ads relevant to your interests (personalized advertising).

You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID

Opting out does not remove ads; it means the ads shown may be less relevant to you.

Data Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your data. To submit a data deletion request, contact us at clyrex9@gmail.com. We will process your request within 30 days.

Data Retention

We do not operate our own servers for storing personal data. Data collected by third-party services is retained according to their respective policies. Transaction records may be retained as required by applicable law.

Children's Privacy

Our Apps are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through our Apps, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to remove such information.

Data Security

All data transmitted by our Apps is encrypted in transit using secure connections (HTTPS/TLS). We rely on Google's infrastructure for data storage and processing, which applies industry-standard security measures.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
