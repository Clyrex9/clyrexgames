Advertising

Our Apps display ads provided by Google AdMob. Google may use device identifiers and usage data to show ads that are relevant to your interests (personalized advertising).

You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID (or "Opt out of Ads Personalization" on older versions)

Opting out does not remove ads from the App; it means the ads shown may be less relevant to you.

Data Retention

We do not retain any personal data on our own servers. Any data collected by third-party services is subject to their respective privacy policies and retention schedules. Please refer to the links above for details.

Our Apps are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. We are committed to protecting your information. Since we do not collect or store personal data directly, your data security depends primarily on the third-party services listed above, which follow industry-standard security practices.
